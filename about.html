Shiny Odds Modifiers

Hypermode

Hypermode allows you to increase your chain to 40, instead of 39. This increases your shiny odds while chaining from 1/400 to 1/200.

Shiny Charm

Shiny Charms multiply your odds by 2.5 times. They last 24 hours.

Gold Amulet

The Gold Amulet multiplies your odds by 1.25 times. It is a permanent reward from achieving Ace Rank.

Pokemon of the Day

Hatching the Pokemon of the Day multiplies your odds by 1.1 times. It is a random, hatchable Pokemon daily.

Sei Power

This boost is more complex than a simple multiplier. It can be determined by the following formula:
Shiny Boost = 1/(sqrt(Initial Shiny Odds) * sqrt(50-(Sei Power)))

Provided by the Official PFQ Wiki

Albino Odds Modifiers

Albino Radar

At least level 1 charge is required to obtain Albinos, and therefore Melanistic Pokemon, at all. It has seven levels, with each level giving you a different base odds.
They are as follows:

  1. 1: 1/6144
  2. 2: 1/3072
  3. 3: 1/1536
  4. 4: 1/768
  5. 5: 1/480
  6. 6: 1/270
  7. 7: 1/180

Silver Amulet

The Silver Amulet multiplies your odds by 1 + 1/3 times. It is a permanent reward from achieving Ultra Rank.

The multiplier for this really is 1 + 1/3, NOT 1.33. It's weird.

Z-Crystal

Z-Crystals multiply your odds by 1.5 times, assuming the hatched Pokemon matches the type of crystal used.

Type Race Buff / Stellar V-Wave

The Stellar V-Wave multiplies your odds by 1.2 times. It is often referred to as the Type Race Buff, as it is only active during Type Races.

Melanistic Odds Modifiers

How are these applied?

Multipliers or buffs to melanistic Pokemon chances are applied AFTER you successfully roll for a Shiny.
They are applied directly to your Albino odds, increasing the chances by the listed multipliers.

When an egg is hatched, the game rolls for a shiny; if it succeeds, it rolls for Albino with the melanistic buffs applied. If it succeeds, you get a Melanistic. If this fails, you get a Shiny.
If the Shiny roll fails, roll for an Albino. If this succeeds, you get an Albino. If it fails, it is normal.

Ubercharm

The Ubercharm multiplies your melanistic odds by 6 times.

Cobalt Amulet

The Cobalt Amulet provides a dynamic multiplier, based on your current chain. It is a reward from obtaining Arceus Rank.
This reward is NOT permanent; you must maintain Arceus Rank or better to continue using it.

The multiplier is an additional 1.015 times for every 100 eggs hatched, up to 1.75 times (which is reached at a chain of 5,000).
